## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Sign Up**
   - Enter your email address
   - Choose a unique username
   - Create a strong password (at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and numbers)

2. **Create Your Character**
   - Choose your character name
   - Select your class (optional)
   - Your adventure begins at Level 1!

### Your First Task

1. Click **"Create Task"**
2. Enter a task title (e.g., "Morning Exercise")
3. Add a description (optional)
4. Choose difficulty:
   - **Easy**: Simple tasks (50 XP)
   - **Medium**: Moderate effort (100 XP)
   - **Hard**: Challenging goals (200 XP)
4. Select task type:
   - **Daily**: Resets every day
   - **Weekly**: Resets every week
   - **One-Time**: Complete once
5. Click **"Create"**

### Completing Your First Task

1. Do the task in real life
2. Click the **checkmark** next to the task
3. Watch your XP increase!
4. See if you earned any items
5. Read your generated story (if available)

## Streaks & Bonuses

### What Are Streaks?

A **streak** is the number of consecutive days or weeks you've completed a task.

### Daily Streaks ğŸ”¥

- Complete a daily task every day
- Each consecutive day increases your streak
- Miss one day = streak resets to 0
- **Bonus**: +10% XP per streak day (max +100%)

**Example**:
- Day 1: 100 XP (no bonus)
- Day 2: 110 XP (+10% bonus)
- Day 3: 120 XP (+20% bonus)
- Day 7: 170 XP (+70% bonus)
- Day 10+: 200 XP (+100% max bonus)

### Weekly Streaks ğŸ“Š

- Complete a weekly task every week
- Consecutive weeks build your streak
- Miss one week = streak resets
- **Bonus**: Same as daily (+10% per week)

### Maintaining Streaks

**Tips for Success**:
1. Set reminders on your phone
2. Complete tasks early in the day
3. Start with easy tasks to build momentum
4. Don't break the chain!

**Grace Period**:
- You have until midnight in your timezone
- Plan ahead for busy days
- Consistency is key

### Streak Recovery

If you break a streak:
- Don't give up!
- Start fresh the next day
- Learn from the miss
- Build an even longer streak

## Tips & Strategies

### For Beginners

1. **Start Small**: Begin with 2-3 easy daily tasks
2. **Be Consistent**: Focus on building streaks
3. **Celebrate Wins**: Enjoy each level up
4. **Read Stories**: They make it more fun!
5. **Don't Overwhelm**: Add tasks gradually

### For Intermediate Players

1. **Mix Difficulties**: Balance easy, medium, and hard tasks
2. **Optimize XP**: Focus on hard tasks with streaks
3. **Complete Weeklies**: Don't forget weekly tasks
4. **Track Progress**: Monitor your stats
5. **Set Goals**: Aim for specific levels

### For Advanced Players

1. **Max Streaks**: Maintain 10+ day streaks
2. **Hard Tasks Only**: Focus on 200 XP tasks
3. **Perfect Weeks**: Complete all tasks every week
4. **Collect Legendaries**: Hunt for rare items
5. **Share Stories**: Inspire others

- **Morning Routine**: Stack easy tasks in the morning
- **Task Batching**: Group similar tasks together
- **Reward Timing**: Complete hard tasks when energized
- **Streak Protection**: Never skip two days in a row
- **Weekly Planning**: Plan your week on Sunday
